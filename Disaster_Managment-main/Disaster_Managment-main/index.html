Disaster Management

If you fail to prepare, prepare to fail

Disaster could be a serious disruption of the functioning of a community or society, that involve widespread human, material, economic or environmental impacts that exceed the power of the affected community or society to cope exploitation its own resources.

A disaster happens once a hazard impacts on vulnerable folks. The mix of hazards, vulnerability and inability to scale back the potential negative consequences of risk ends up in disaster.

Disaster management is how we deal with the human material, economic or environmental impacts of said disaster, it's the method of however we have a tendency to “Prepare for, reply to and learn from the consequences of major failures”.

Whether Natural or Artificial , children are the foremost vulnerable once a disaster strikes. Quite 1/2 the people that are littered with disasters are children. It's our job to safeguard the well-being of children affected with disasters.

Disaster Management because the organisation and management of resources and responsibilities for managing all the humanitarian aspects of emergencies, specially preparation, response and recovery so as to minimize the impact of disasters.

We work with families and people additionally as native, national partners across the country to confirm communities are conscious of the risks they face and the way to properly prepare and respond within the event of an emergency.
